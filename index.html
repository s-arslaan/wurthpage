Features

Built to make material flow feel effortless

From sensor signal to delivered bin, Final Meter keeps everyone aligned with lean, auditable steps and delightful speed.

01

Workplaces & Article Assignment

Model your sinks, assign items, choose container sizes and source priorities, then set precise shelf coordinates.

02

Demand Lifecycle

Every scan raises a demand and tracks it through clear states—so nothing stalls or goes missing.

03

Stock, Bins & States

See Ready, In Delivery, In Use, and Empty at a glance, per bin and per location.

04

Delivery Controls

Pick to cart, load to train, deliver in bulk or direct, confirm via scans—your rules, your routes.

05

Routes & Trips

Define sequences of workplaces and storages, then execute trips with only the stops that currently need attention.

06

Mobile: Pick • Load • Deliver

Guided, scan‑first workflows keep the floor fast and accurate—errors drop, throughput climbs.
